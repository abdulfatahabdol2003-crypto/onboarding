Lagos — Builder Bootcamp

Overview

An intensive 3-day bootcamp designed to take you from Web3 beginner to confident smart contract developer. Learn Solidity, deploy contracts, and build real-world dApps with hands-on mentorship from industry experts.

Agenda

  • Day 1: Blockchain fundamentals, Solidity basics, and development environment setup
  • Day 2: Smart contract patterns, testing, and security best practices
  • Day 3: Build and deploy your first dApp, final presentations, and networking

Speakers & Mentors

  • Chioma Okafor - Lead Blockchain Engineer at Polygon
  • David Chen - Smart Contract Auditor
  • Amara Nwosu - DeFi Protocol Founder

Location

📍 CcHUB, 294 Herbert Macaulay Way, Yaba, Lagos, Nigeria

View on Google Maps →

Prerequisites

  • Basic programming knowledge (JavaScript preferred)
  • Laptop with development environment
  • Verified ONBOARD3 Student status
